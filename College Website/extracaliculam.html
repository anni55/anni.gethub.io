Function

College events and functions are an important aspect of college life, providing students with opportunities to engage with their peers, learn new skills, and have fun. From academic conferences and cultural festivals to athletic competitions and social gatherings, there are a wide variety of events that take place on college life throughout the year. These events bring students together and create unity in students.

Seminar

A seminar is an academic event or class that focuses on a particular topic or theme. In college, seminars are a way for students to engage in in-depth discussions about a subject with their peers and professors. These classes often require a significant amount of preparation and participation from the students, and can vary in format from traditional lectures to more interactive discussions.One of the primary benefits of seminars is that they encourage students to take an active role in their learning.

Digital weak

A digital week in college is an event that aims to increase awareness and understanding of technology and its impact on society. It is a week-long program of activities, workshops, and talks designed to educate students on various aspects of digital technology.The purpose of a digital week is to help students develop a better understanding of how technology affects their lives and how they can use it to their advantage.

Farewall

Farewell in college is an emotional event that marks the end of a student's journey in college. It is a time to celebrate the achievements and memories made during their academic journey, and to bid farewell to friends, faculty, and staff who have been a part of their college experience.The farewell event usually takes place during the last week of college before the final exams. The event is organized by the college administration, student council, or a group of junior students. The venue for the farewell is our seminar hall.
